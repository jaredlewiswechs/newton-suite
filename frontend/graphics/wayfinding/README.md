# Newton Wayfinding Graphics

Marketing and identity graphics for Newton Supercomputer, inspired by the **PANYNJ (Port Authority of New York and New Jersey) Wayfinding Manual**.

## Design Inspiration

The PANYNJ Wayfinding Manual represents world-class wayfinding design, winning the 2021 SEGD Honor Award. Key principles applied to Newton:

- **Bold, dimensional beacon-style signage** - Faceted 3D sign boxes recognizable from any angle
- **NYC sleek black backgrounds** - Clean, sophisticated base for vibrant colors
- **Evolved Helvetica typography** - Bold, performant wayfinding typeface
- **Color-coded destination system** - Intuitive navigation through complex spaces
- **Pictogram-based communication** - Universal symbols for quick recognition
- **No-nonsense personality** - Bold, energetic, confident

## Typography

- **Primary**: Helvetica Neue (Bold, Black weights)
- **Monospace**: SF Mono / Consolas (for code)
- **Letter-spacing**: Generous tracking for wayfinding clarity

## The Newton-Ada Relationship

Newton is the verified computation engine, and Ada Computing Company is the organization that built it. The graphics explore this relationship:

- Newton as the **active computation** (teal, dynamic)
- Ada as the **foundational origin** (gold, heritage)
