## Technical Specifications

### Recording Setup
- **Device**: iPhone 14 or later (for screen recording quality)
- **Resolution**: 1170 x 2532 (native iPhone resolution)
- **Frame rate**: 60fps (for smooth UI transitions)
- **Format**: MOV or MP4
- **Orientation**: Portrait (vertical video)

### Screen Recording Settings
1. Settings â†’ Control Center
2. Add "Screen Recording"
3. Swipe down from top-right
4. Long-press Screen Recording
5. Enable "Microphone" (for voiceover)
6. Tap "Start Recording"

### Editing
- **Software**: iMovie (iOS), Final Cut Pro (Mac), or Premiere Pro
- **Transitions**: Fast cuts, no fades (keeps energy up)
- **Text overlays**: Green monospace font (terminal aesthetic)
- **Background**: Keep iPhone screen full frame, no borders

### Audio
- **Voiceover**: Clear, confident, not rushed
- **Microphone**: AirPods Pro or better (clear audio essential)
- **Background music**: Optional, very low volume if used
- **Sound effects**: Subtle "beep" on BLOCKED, "ding" on VERIFIED
