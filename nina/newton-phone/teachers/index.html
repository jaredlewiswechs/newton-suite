Understanding NES Phases

Opening (5 min)

Hook students with a real-world connection. Share the learning objective. Students restate in their own words.

Direct Instruction (15 min)

Teacher models 2-3 examples using think-aloud strategy. Check for understanding every 3-5 minutes.

Guided Practice (15 min)

Collaborative practice in pairs or small groups. Teacher circulates and provides immediate feedback.

Independent Practice (10 min)

Students work individually on 5-8 problems. This serves as formative assessment data.

Closing (5 min)

Exit ticket with 2-3 questions. Use results to determine tomorrow's reteach groups.

Tips for Best Results

  • Be specific with TEKS: Enter the exact TEKS codes for better alignment.
  • Use the topic field: Adding a specific topic improves the lesson focus.
  • Include accommodations: List students who need ELL, 504, SPED, or GT support.
  • Analyze exit tickets promptly: Use the Assessment Analyzer immediately after class.
  • Generate PLC reports weekly: Keep your team informed with regular data updates.
