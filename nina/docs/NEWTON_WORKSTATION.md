## Why This Matters

**HyperCard let anyone make software by moving cards around.**

**Newton Workstation lets anyone use verified AI by clicking apps.**

The constraint IS the instruction. The verification IS the computation. The workstation IS the interface.

You don't need to understand CDL. You don't need to write JSON. You don't need to call APIs.

You open Calculator. You calculate. It's verified.
You open Clipper. You ask something. It negotiates.
You open Canvas. You describe something. It draws.
